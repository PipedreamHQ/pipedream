# Overview

[Trolley](https://trolley.com) is a global payouts platform that lets businesses send payments to recipients worldwide. The Trolley API on Pipedream lets you automate the full payouts lifecycle — creating batches, managing recipients, generating invoices, and triggering payments — all without manual intervention. You can connect Trolley to hundreds of other apps on Pipedream to build end-to-end payment workflows.

# Example Use Cases

- **Automate Contractor Payouts**: When a contractor marks work as complete in a project management tool like Jira or Asana, a Pipedream workflow can automatically create a payment batch in Trolley and add a payment for that contractor. This removes manual steps from your accounts payable process and ensures contractors are paid on time.

- **Invoice-to-Payment Pipeline**: When a new invoice is approved in your billing system (e.g., QuickBooks or Google Sheets), trigger a Pipedream workflow that creates a matching invoice in Trolley and immediately creates an invoice payment against it. The result is sent back to your system, keeping your records in sync automatically.

- **Payment Status Alerts**: After triggering batch processing in Trolley, use Pipedream to poll for batch completion and send a Slack or email notification to your finance team with a summary of successful and failed payments. This gives your team real-time visibility into payout status without logging into the Trolley dashboard.
